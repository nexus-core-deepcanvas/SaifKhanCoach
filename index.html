About the Coach

Saif Khan

Strength Coach · Personal Trainer · Life Coach

Saif Khan has spent the last five years coaching clients across India — from gym floors in Satna and Mumbai to remote clients worldwide through FITTR. He has worked under established brands like Curefit (House of Cult), Anytime Fitness, and BOMISO GYM & SPA, building a reputation as a coach who delivers real, measurable results.

His approach goes beyond sets and reps. Saif integrates structured strength programming, intelligent conditioning, practical nutrition, and life coaching into a single, cohesive system — because he knows the gym is only one piece of the puzzle.

Power Rack Zone

3 full power racks with Olympic barbells and plates up to 220 kg. Built for squats, bench, deadlifts, and every major compound lift.

💪

Free Weights

Full dumbbell rack from 2 kg to 60 kg, EZ bars, and a dedicated floor space for accessory work and supersets.

Cable & Machines

Dual cable crossover station, lat pulldown, seated row, leg press, and a full range of plate-loaded machines.

Cardio Zone

Functional Turf

20-foot indoor turf strip for sled pushes, battle ropes, kettlebell circuits, and functional conditioning drills.

Changing Rooms

Clean, air-conditioned locker rooms with secure storage so you can train straight from work or head out right after.
